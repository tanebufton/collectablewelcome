Welcome to Collectable

We're excited to have you as part of the team - Before you begin there is two simple steps you must complete. 

 

Step 1. Link your Discord account 

 

As Collectable is a discord based service, you must make sure that your membership is linked to your discord account - This way you'll have access to all of our features and functions.

 

After linking your Discord, you'll be automatically added into the server

 Step 2. Read our Server Rules

 

Once you are in the server, please make sure that you have read and understand the server rules, including those around sharing information and account sharing. 

Step 3. Read our guides to learn all about Collectable

 

Using discord for the first time and not knowing what to keep an eye out for can be overwhelming at first - We've created a handy set guides to help you understand how to add your roles, what channels to keep an eye out for and where all the information you need will be posted.
